FAQ

FAQs about social media marketing

What is social media marketing?
Social media marketing is the practice of promoting your business on social media platforms to increase customer attraction and build your brand. Social media marketing typically involves creating posts, responding to comments and monitoring interactions.
Why is social media marketing important?

Social media marketing is important for several reasons, including:

86% of people use social media platforms at least once a day
74% of people use social media when making purchasing decisions
59% of marketers use social media for lead generation and sales

How do I market my business on social media platforms?

When it comes to how to market your business on social media, you need to follow these steps:

• Define your goals to learn what you want to achieve with social media

• Research your audience to identify their preferred platforms

• Define your strategy for how to achieve your social media goals

• Build your creatives, for your social media posts

• Engage your audience through by listening to and responding to feedback and troubleshooting problems

• Measure your performance to determine if you are achieving your goals on social networks

• Identify your next steps to improve your performance on social networks
